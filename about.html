How GENCARZ begun

GENCARZ began as a small car repair shop in 1997. It was then named FC Auto Repair Shop and was located within the premises of Mr. FC’s residence in Poblacion East. FC were the initials of the owner and financier. He financed the purchase of all necessary tools and equipment for machine shop and welding works. FC’s first repair jobs were done on tricycles, kuliglig, owner jeepneys. Then repairs on cars started being done too.
In 1999, Mr. FC, a seaman by profession, was called back by his maritime company and was given an offer he couldn’t refuse. So he decided to accept the offer. He entrusted the shop to his friend, Mr. Jun Romero who at that time was the shop’s chief mechanic.
In 1999, Mr. Romero decided to transfer the shop to his late grandmother’s old house in Poblacion West. There were 3 main reasons for his decision to relocate. The first one being the strategic location of Grandma’s old house. It was in the corner of 2 main thoroughfares – Ramos and Bauzon Streets. Second was to save on rent since the old house belonged to Mr. Romero’s grandmother. And the third reason was that it was more spacious than the previous location.
Mr. Romero decided to rename the shop GENCARZ Auto Repair Shop. The shop started other jobs like car painting, carwashing and auto detailing. Its clientele kept growing so much so that during the construction of the San Roque Dam in San Manuel in the late 1990’s , the contractor commissioned GENCARZ to take charge of the repair and maintenance of its service vehicles and even some of its heavy equipment. Auto restoration, carwash and auto detailing were also added to its services. GENCARZ’s advertisers were its satisfied customers who spread the good word of quality and dependable service. Its customer base extended to neighboring towns and provinces. One of the satisfied customers of GENCARZ was San Miguel PBA star Danny Ildefonso who brought in his vintage VW Beetle for restoration.
The moment of truth for GENCARZ’S sterling service came when it joined its first ever Luzon Carshow held at Fort Bonifacio parade grounds in 2006. It had 3 entries and all entries garnered top ranks. Overall champion was GENCARZ because it garnered the most coveted Best of Show trophy. Since then GENCARZ became a name to reckon with in auto restoration. GENCARZ joined the biggest carshow held at SMEX in December 2021. His entry was a vintage 1974 VW Beetle owned by former governor of Pangasinan, Atty. Rafael Colet. It was declared the Best of Show. Once again another laurel for GENCARZ. For winning the highest award, GENCARZ was featured in Top Gear Magazine and Mr. Jum was even interviewed by GMA Balitang Amianan.
Today, GENCARZ has 10 regular employees in the shop. The rest is history.
